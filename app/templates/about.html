About OKCaddie

🏌️‍♂️ Our Mission

OKCaddie is dedicated to helping global golfers discover the hidden gems and world-class championship courses across Japan. From the majestic views of Mt. Fuji to the challenging coastal links, we provide a curated map to make your Japanese golf trip unforgettable.

🌟 What We Provide

  • Interactive Golf Map: Easily find courses near your destination using our visual map interface.
  • Detailed Course Guides: Deep dives into course layouts, signature holes, facility conditions, and access information.
  • Theme-Based Filtering: Quickly find courses by type—Scenic Views, Championship layouts, Resort stays, or Links-style.
  • Booking Integration: Direct links to help you secure your tee times at the best available rates.
