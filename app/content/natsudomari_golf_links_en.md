Natsudomari Golf Links in Aomori is one of Japan’s clearest **Scottish-style seaside links** experiences—firm turf, coastal wind, and deep bunkers overlooking Mutsu Bay. It is a bucket-list day for players who like ground game and weather over resort polish.

## Quick facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Location** | Hiranai, Aomori (Natsudomari Peninsula) |
| **Style** | Seaside links |
| **Signature** | Wind, firm fairways, pot-style bunkers |
| **Visitor access** | Often bookable in season via major portals |
| **Best months** | June–September (shoulder can be windy/cold) |
| **Best for** | Links lovers, mid+ handicappers who manage trajectory |

## Who should play

- You want **Japan links**, not a tree-lined parkland clone
- You are OK with wind ruining a “perfect” scorecard
- You will travel to Tohoku for one special round

If you need calm resort golf with ocean photos only, look at Okinawa resorts instead.

## How it plays

Keep the ball **low and in play**. Club up into the wind, club down downwind, and avoid short-siding elevated greens. Bunkers are penal—lay up short of fairway pots when the line is greedy. On calm rare days the course feels wide; on typical days it feels half a club longer and a fairway tighter.

## Green fees & booking

Tohoku links packages vary widely. Plan roughly **mid-¥10,000s to ¥20,000s+** for visitor products, higher on weekends. Search [booking](/booking/natsudomari_golf_links_en), confirm inclusions, and add rentals early—specialty sets sell out on holiday weeks.

## Access

- **Aomori Airport (AOJ)** or **Misawa (MSJ)** + rental car
- Drive times depend on which airport; build 60–90 minutes buffer from AOJ-area hotels for morning tees
- Public transit to the peninsula is awkward for golf bags—drive

Combine with Aomori city / Oirase sightseeing only if your tee time allows; don’t squeeze a dawn round after a late arrival.

## Dress & tips

- Soft spikes; layered windproof clothing even in summer
- Soft spikes and a rain shell in the bag year-round
- Related: [Hokkaido summer golf](/guide/hokkaido-summer-golf) for northern season planning

## Bottom line

Natsudomari is for golfers searching **“Natsudomari Golf Links tee times”** who actually want links golf. Book a windy day on purpose, keep ego out of club selection, and enjoy Mutsu Bay when the ball is in the fairway.
