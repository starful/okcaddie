**Access:**

Natsudomari Golf Links is located in Hiranai-machi, Aomori Prefecture. The most convenient way to reach the course is by car. From Aomori City, take the Aomori Expressway towards Mutsu. Exit at the Hiranai Interchange and follow the signs for Natsudomari Golf Links. The journey takes approximately 1 hour. Alternatively, you can take a train to Noheji Station on the Aoimori Railway and then take a taxi to the golf course (approximately 20 minutes). While public transport is an option, a car is highly recommended for exploring the surrounding area and enjoying the scenic beauty of Aomori Prefecture. Rental cars are readily available at Aomori Airport and Aomori Station. The course provides ample parking for visitors. The drive to the course itself is a pleasant experience, offering glimpses of the local countryside.
