**Price & Booking:**

Weekday green fees at Eniwa Country Club typically range from ¥25,000 to ¥35,000 JPY, while weekend and holiday rates can climb to ¥40,000 to ¥55,000 JPY. These prices generally include green fees, caddie fees (which are often mandatory), and consumption tax. Booking in advance is highly recommended, especially during peak seasons (late spring to early autumn). Consider booking a stay & play package for a more comprehensive and cost-effective experience, as many local hotels partner with the club to offer discounted rates and guaranteed tee times. Early spring (May-June) and late autumn (September-October) often offer the best value, as the weather is still pleasant, and the crowds are thinner. Booking through official Japanese golf booking websites or directly with the club is generally the most reliable way to secure your tee time. Pro-tip: check for seasonal promotions or weekday discounts to potentially save on green fees.

**Course Highlights:**

The course at Eniwa Country Club is a masterpiece of strategic design, thoughtfully crafted to challenge golfers of all skill levels. While the architect's name isn't widely publicized, the course's distinct character speaks volumes about the design philosophy. The layout features a harmonious blend of undulating fairways, strategically placed bunkers, and meticulously maintained greens. The signature holes are often cited as the par-3 17th, which demands a precise tee shot over a water hazard to a well-guarded green, and the par-5 18th, a challenging finishing hole that rewards accuracy and strategic decision-making. The course's length and elevation changes provide a good test of driving distance and club selection. Expect challenging approach shots, especially into the well-bunkered greens. The greens themselves are known for their smooth surfaces and subtle breaks, requiring a keen eye and a deft touch with the putter.

**Access:**

Eniwa Country Club is conveniently located approximately 45 minutes by car from New Chitose Airport (CTS), the main international airport serving Hokkaido. From the airport, take the Hokkaido Expressway and follow the signs to Eniwa. Alternatively, you can take a train from New Chitose Airport Station to Eniwa Station, which takes approximately 30 minutes. From Eniwa Station, you can take a taxi to the golf club, which is approximately a 15-minute ride. Rental cars are readily available at the airport and are the recommended mode of transportation for those who wish to explore the surrounding area. The drive to the club is scenic and well-marked.
