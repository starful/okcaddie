**Access:**

Reaching Chukyo Golf Club requires careful planning, as it is primarily accessible by car due to its location.

*   **From Nagoya Station:** The most convenient route is by car. Take the Tomei Expressway towards Toyota. Exit at the Toyota IC (Interchange). From there, follow local roads towards the golf club. The journey typically takes around 60-90 minutes, depending on traffic.
*   **Public Transportation:** While possible, using public transport is cumbersome and not recommended. It would involve taking a train from Nagoya Station to Toyota Station, followed by a lengthy taxi ride to the golf club. This option is time-consuming and expensive.
*   **Important Note:** As a private club, it's crucial to inform the club of your arrival time in advance, especially if you are a guest of a member. Clear directions and instructions will be provided by the member hosting you.
