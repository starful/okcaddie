Unlike many of the nation's publicly accessible courses, Eagle Point cultivates an atmosphere of refined tranquility and unparalleled privacy. The experience begins long before the first tee shot, with access strictly reserved for members and their invited guests, adding an extra layer of prestige and exclusivity to every visit. This unwavering commitment to privacy allows for meticulously maintained course conditions and a heightened level of personalized service that sets Eagle Point apart from the rest. Eagle Point is more than just a golf course; it is a sanctuary where the pursuit of golfing excellence is intertwined with moments of serene contemplation and connection.

While specific membership fees are not publicly disclosed, they are undoubtedly substantial, reflecting the club's commitment to providing a premium and exclusive golfing experience. **Access:**

Eagle Point Golf Club is located in Ushiku, Ibaraki Prefecture, approximately one hour by car from central Tokyo.

*   **By Car:** Take the Joban Expressway to the Ushiku-Ami Interchange. From the interchange, follow the signs to Eagle Point Golf Club. The drive is relatively straightforward and well-signposted.
*   **By Train:** Take the JR Joban Line to Ushiku Station. From Ushiku Station, a taxi ride to Eagle Point Golf Club will take approximately 20 minutes. Pre-arranging a taxi is recommended, especially during peak hours.
*   **From Nearest Major City:** The nearest major city is Tokyo. The journey from Tokyo to Eagle Point Golf Club is relatively easy by car or train.
