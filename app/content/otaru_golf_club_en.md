**Price & Booking:**

Otaru Golf Club falls into the premium price range, reflecting the quality of the course and the services provided. Expect weekday green fees to range from ¥25,000 to ¥35,000 per player, while weekend rates typically climb to ¥35,000 to ¥50,000. These prices are estimates and can fluctuate depending on the season and availability. It's crucial to book well in advance, especially during peak season (May-June and September-October), to secure your preferred tee time. Many golfers opt for stay-and-play packages through partnered hotels, which often offer discounted rates and guaranteed access to the course. Booking directly through the official Otaru Golf Club website is also recommended, as it provides the most up-to-date information on pricing and availability. Note that prices often include caddy fees, which are practically mandatory. Gratuity is appreciated for excellent service.

**Course Highlights:**

The course at Otaru Golf Club is a masterpiece of strategic design, thoughtfully laid out to challenge players of all skill levels. While the architect's name isn't widely publicized, the course's layout speaks volumes about the expertise involved. The 18-hole course is characterized by its undulating fairways, strategically placed bunkers, and meticulously maintained greens. Water hazards come into play on several holes, demanding precision and careful club selection.

Signature holes include:

*   **Hole 5 (Par 4):** A visually stunning hole with the Sea of Japan as a backdrop. The tee shot requires accuracy to avoid fairway bunkers, while the approach shot demands precision to a well-protected green. The view alone makes this hole memorable.

*   **Hole 9 (Par 5):** A challenging par 5 that requires a well-placed drive to set up a second shot over a water hazard. The green is guarded by bunkers, demanding a precise approach.

*   **Hole 18 (Par 4):** A demanding finishing hole that requires a precise tee shot to avoid a strategically placed fairway bunker. The approach shot must navigate a challenging green complex, making par a rewarding finish.

The course rewards strategic play over brute force, demanding careful course management and precise shot-making. The undulating terrain and strategically placed hazards create a challenging yet rewarding golfing experience.

**Access:**

Otaru Golf Club is conveniently located near the city of Otaru, Hokkaido. The most convenient way to access the club is by car.

*   **From Sapporo:** Take the Hokkaido Expressway towards Otaru. Exit at the Otaru IC and follow the signs to Otaru Golf Club. The drive takes approximately 45-60 minutes, depending on traffic conditions.

*   **From New Chitose Airport (CTS):** Take the JR Rapid Airport train to Otaru Station. From Otaru Station, you can take a taxi to Otaru Golf Club, which takes about 20-30 minutes. Alternatively, arrange for a private transfer from the airport to the golf club.

Detailed driving directions and public transportation information can be found on the Otaru Golf Club website.
