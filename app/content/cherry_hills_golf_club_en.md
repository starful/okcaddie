Located in the heart of Hyogo Prefecture, an area renowned for its golfing pedigree, Cherry Hills exudes an air of exclusivity and understated elegance. From the moment you arrive, you are enveloped in a world of manicured greens, challenging fairways, and unparalleled service. Forget bustling tourist traps; Cherry Hills is about refined play and a deep appreciation for the game. This is a haven for those seeking a truly exceptional and private golfing escape.

**Price & Booking:**

As a private club, Cherry Hills Golf Club does not offer publicly available green fees. Access is almost exclusively limited to members and their accompanied guests. Guests will be subject to a guest fee, which, while not publicly advertised, is consistent with a premium golfing experience in Japan – anticipate a range significantly higher than public courses. The price reflects the exclusivity, the impeccable course maintenance, and the superior level of service. Peak seasons, such as spring (cherry blossom season) and autumn (foliage season), may see even higher guest fees if available at all. Booking for guests is handled entirely through the member who is sponsoring the visit. Direct contact with the club for tee times is not possible unless you are a member.

**Facilities:**

Cherry Hills Golf Club boasts world-class facilities that complement the exceptional golfing experience. The clubhouse is a testament to refined elegance, offering a welcoming and sophisticated atmosphere. The pro shop is stocked with a curated selection of high-end golf equipment, apparel, and accessories. However, the standout facility is undoubtedly the Onsen (hot spring bath). After a challenging round, members and their guests can relax and rejuvenate in the traditional Japanese Onsen, complete with indoor and outdoor bathing areas. The Onsen offers a tranquil escape, providing the perfect opportunity to soothe tired muscles and reflect on the day's play. The quality of the Onsen is exceptional, rivaling that of many high-end spas. It is a truly luxurious amenity that elevates the entire Cherry Hills experience. The clubhouse also offers private rooms for meetings or more intimate dining experiences. Locker rooms are spacious and well-appointed, providing a comfortable and convenient space to prepare for and unwind after a round of golf.

**Scenic Beauty:**

Cherry Hills Golf Club is located in an area of outstanding natural beauty. The course is surrounded by rolling hills, lush forests, and picturesque valleys. The best time to visit is during the spring (sakura season) or autumn (koyo season) when the surrounding landscape is awash in vibrant colors. The cherry blossoms in spring create a breathtaking spectacle, while the autumn foliage transforms the hills into a tapestry of reds, oranges, and yellows. Even during the summer months, the lush greenery provides a refreshing escape from the heat.

The views from the course are simply stunning. On a clear day, you can see for miles in all directions. The sunsets are particularly spectacular, painting the sky with fiery hues. The natural beauty of the area adds an extra dimension to the golfing experience, creating a sense of tranquility and serenity. The course is meticulously maintained to blend seamlessly with the surrounding landscape, enhancing the overall aesthetic appeal.

**Access:**

Access to Cherry Hills Golf Club requires careful planning, particularly for non-members. The club is located in Miki, Hyogo Prefecture. The most convenient way to reach the club is typically by car.

*   **From Osaka:** Take the Chugoku Expressway towards Kobe. Exit at the Miki-Higashi Interchange. Follow the signs for Cherry Hills Golf Club (カーナビ利用推奨). The drive typically takes around 1 hour.
*   **From Kobe:** Take the Sanyo Expressway towards Okayama. Exit at the Miki-Ono Interchange. Follow the signs for Cherry Hills Golf Club (カーナビ利用推奨). The drive typically takes around 45 minutes.

While public transportation options exist, they are significantly less convenient and would involve a combination of train and taxi or bus travel. It's highly recommended to rent a car or arrange for private transportation to ensure a smooth and timely arrival. Note that car navigation systems in Japan are generally very reliable and will provide accurate directions to the club. As a private club, ensuring you have proper confirmation of your tee time through your sponsoring member is absolutely critical before making the journey.
