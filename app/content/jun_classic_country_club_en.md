**Price & Booking:**

Green fees at Jun Classic Country Club can be considered in the upper-mid range, reflecting the quality of the course and the services offered. Weekday green fees typically range from ¥18,000 to ¥25,000 (approximately $120-$170 USD), while weekend and holiday rates can climb to ¥28,000 to ¥35,000 (approximately $190-$240 USD) or more, depending on the season and specific packages. These fees usually include golf cart rental, but it's always best to confirm at the time of booking.

Seasonal tips: Spring (March-May) and Autumn (September-November) are the peak seasons, offering the most pleasant weather and stunning scenery. Booking well in advance (at least a month or two) is highly recommended, especially for weekends and holidays. Consider visiting during the shoulder seasons (early June or late August) for potentially lower rates and fewer crowds. Keep an eye out for special promotions or packages offered through the club's website or affiliated travel agencies. Playing on a weekday will almost always give you a lower rate.

Booking can be done via the official Jun Classic Country Club website, through reputable golf travel agencies specializing in Japanese golf tours, or by contacting the club directly by phone (Japanese language proficiency might be required for phone bookings). Many online golf booking platforms are increasingly offering options for booking tee times at courses like Jun Classic, so that is also worth investigating.

**Access:**

Getting to Jun Classic Country Club requires some planning, but the journey is well worth the effort.

*   **By Train:** The nearest major train station is Utsunomiya Station on the JR Tohoku Shinkansen line. From Utsunomiya Station, take a local train to Nasushiobara Station (approx. 20 minutes). From Nasushiobara Station, a taxi ride to Jun Classic Country Club will take approximately 40-50 minutes, costing around ¥8,000 - ¥10,000.
*   **By Car:** The most convenient way to reach Jun Classic Country Club is by car. From Tokyo, take the Tohoku Expressway and exit at the Yaita IC. From the Yaita IC, follow the signs for Jun Classic Country Club (approx. 30 minutes). Car navigation systems (GPS) are highly recommended. Parking is readily available at the club.
*   **From Narita (NRT) or Haneda (HND) Airport:** Take the Narita Express (N'EX) or Limousine Bus from Narita Airport to Tokyo Station. From Haneda Airport, take the Keikyu Line or Limousine Bus to Tokyo Station. From Tokyo Station, take the Tohoku Shinkansen to Utsunomiya Station and follow the directions above.
