From the moment you arrive, you are enveloped in an atmosphere of understated elegance and impeccable service. The club caters to golfers who appreciate the finer things in life, offering a meticulously maintained course, world-class facilities, and a commitment to providing an unforgettable experience. Gifu Seki Country Club isn't merely a place to play golf; it's a destination to savor.

**Price & Booking:**

The pricing at Gifu Seki Country Club reflects its status as a premium golfing destination. Green fees typically range from ¥25,000 to ¥45,000 on weekdays and ¥35,000 to ¥60,000 on weekends and holidays. These prices are estimates and can fluctuate based on the season, package deals, and membership status. It's always recommended to check the official website or contact the club directly for the most up-to-date pricing.

Seasonal Tips: Playing in the spring (March-May) and autumn (September-November) offers the most pleasant weather conditions and stunning scenery as the foliage transforms. Booking well in advance, particularly during these peak seasons, is essential to secure your tee time. Consider booking a package that includes accommodation at a nearby ryokan (traditional Japanese inn) or hotel for a complete "stay & play" experience. Many packages offer discounted rates and additional perks. Booking through a reputable Japanese golf tour operator can also simplify the process and provide access to exclusive deals.

**Course Accessibility:**

Gifu Seki Country Club operates primarily as a semi-private club. While membership provides significant privileges, non-members can typically access the course, subject to availability and higher green fees. Booking well in advance is crucial for non-members. Contacting the club directly or utilizing a specialized booking service that caters to foreign golfers is the best way to secure a tee time. While walk-ins might be possible, they are strongly discouraged, especially during peak seasons. Consider inquiring about introduction privileges through existing members if you have any connections within the Japanese golfing community.

**Facilities:**

Gifu Seki Country Club excels in providing world-class facilities to enhance the golfer's experience.

*   **Clubhouse:** The clubhouse is a testament to Japanese elegance, featuring spacious lounges, comfortable seating areas, and panoramic views of the course. It serves as a welcoming haven for golfers to relax and socialize before and after their rounds.

The quality of the Onsen is exceptional, with separate facilities for men and women, featuring both indoor and outdoor baths. *   **Practice Facilities:** The driving range offers ample space for golfers to warm up and hone their skills before their rounds. Practice putting greens and chipping areas are also available, allowing golfers to refine their short game.

**Access:**

Gifu Seki Country Club is located in Seki, Gifu Prefecture.

*   **From Nagoya:** The most convenient way to access the club from Nagoya is by car. The drive takes approximately 1 hour and 15 minutes via the Tokai-Hokuriku Expressway. Take the Mino IC exit and follow the signs to Seki.

*   **By Train:** From Nagoya Station, take the JR Takayama Line to Mino-Ota Station. From Mino-Ota Station, take a taxi to Gifu Seki Country Club (approximately 30 minutes).

It's recommended to rent a car for maximum flexibility and convenience, especially if you plan to explore other attractions in the Gifu Prefecture. Alternatively, pre-arranging a private transfer from Nagoya is also an option, though it can be more expensive.
