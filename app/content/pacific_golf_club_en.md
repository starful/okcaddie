**Price & Booking:**

Pacific Golf Club operates primarily as a private club, granting access to members and their esteemed guests. However, limited opportunities exist for non-members to experience this exceptional course through exclusive stay-and-play packages offered by select partner hotels in the Kochi region. Expect green fees to be in the range of ¥35,000 to ¥55,000 per round, depending on the season and package inclusions. Weekend rates typically command a premium. Securing a tee time requires advance planning and often involves contacting a partner hotel or seeking an introduction from a club member. During peak seasons (spring and autumn), booking well in advance (several months) is highly recommended to avoid disappointment. Consider inquiring about twilight rates for potentially reduced fees during the late afternoon. Remember, availability is limited, underscoring the club’s exclusivity and enhancing the overall experience.

**Access:**

Reaching Pacific Golf Club requires careful planning due to its secluded location. The nearest major city is Kochi City, which is served by Kochi Ryoma Airport (KCZ). From the airport, you can take a taxi or rental car to the club, which is approximately a 45-minute drive. Alternatively, you can take a train to Kochi Station and then transfer to a local bus or taxi to reach the club. Driving directions are as follows: From Kochi City, take Route 55 south towards Konan. Follow Route 55 until you see signs for Pacific Golf Club. The club is located off the main road, so pay close attention to the signage. Given the limited public transport options, renting a car is highly recommended for ease of access and exploring the surrounding area. The club offers ample parking for members and guests. Ensure you have confirmed your tee time reservation before traveling to the club, as access is strictly controlled.
