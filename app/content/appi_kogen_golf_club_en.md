**Introduction:**

Appi Kogen Golf Club, located within the renowned Appi Kogen ski resort area of Iwate Prefecture, offers a golfing experience that seamlessly blends challenge, natural beauty, and Japanese hospitality. Unlike many exclusive, members-only courses, Appi Kogen provides access to the public, making it an ideal choice for both seasoned golfers and those new to the sport seeking an exceptional mountain golfing adventure. The course's design cleverly utilizes the natural terrain, creating a strategic and aesthetically pleasing layout that will test your skills while captivating your senses. The club's commitment to providing an all-encompassing experience, from meticulously maintained greens to top-notch facilities, ensures a memorable visit. It's a testament to Japanese craftsmanship and dedication to customer satisfaction.

**Price & Booking:**

Green fees at Appi Kogen Golf Club vary depending on the season and day of the week. As of late 2024, expect weekday fees to range from ¥8,000 to ¥12,000 per person, while weekend and holiday fees typically fall between ¥12,000 and ¥18,000. Prices may be higher during peak season (July-August and October). It is HIGHLY recommended to book in advance, especially during these peak periods, to secure your preferred tee time. Booking can be done through the Appi Kogen Resort official website, major Japanese online booking platforms, or by contacting the golf club directly via phone. Look out for special packages that combine green fees with accommodation at the Appi Grand Villa or other nearby hotels; these packages often offer significant savings. Off-season golfing (spring and autumn) provides a more budget-friendly option, with potentially lower green fees and fewer crowds.

**Course Accessibility:**

Appi Kogen Golf Club is a public course, welcoming both members and non-members alike. This accessibility is a significant advantage, making it a popular choice for travelling golfers. While membership options may exist (primarily for locals), they are not required to play. The club operates with a focus on providing a high-quality experience to all guests, regardless of membership status. Tee times are readily available for booking, although, as mentioned, advanced booking is strongly advised, particularly during peak seasons and weekends. The user-friendly online booking system simplifies the process, allowing you to plan your golfing adventure with ease.

**Course Highlights:**

The Appi Kogen Golf Club boasts an 18-hole, par-72 course designed to challenge golfers of all skill levels. While not attributed to a single, world-renowned architect, the course layout reflects a thoughtful understanding of the natural landscape and a commitment to strategic design. The course is characterized by its undulating fairways, strategically placed bunkers, and challenging water hazards. Several holes stand out as particularly memorable:

*   **Hole 5 (Par 4):** This hole demands accuracy off the tee, with a narrow fairway flanked by trees and a strategically placed bunker guarding the left side. The approach shot requires precision to navigate a small, elevated green protected by bunkers.

*   **Hole 9 (Par 5):** A reachable par 5 for longer hitters, but fraught with risk. A well-placed tee shot is crucial to avoid the fairway bunkers. The second shot requires a strategic decision: go for the green in two, or lay up to a comfortable distance for your approach. The green is undulating and guarded by water on the right.

*   **Hole 18 (Par 4):** A stunning finishing hole that offers a panoramic view of the surrounding mountains. The tee shot is downhill, requiring careful club selection to avoid overshooting the fairway. The approach shot is played to a well-protected green with the clubhouse as a backdrop. This hole provides a memorable conclusion to your round.

The course is known for its meticulously maintained greens and fairways, providing excellent playing conditions throughout the season. The strategic bunkering and water hazards add an element of challenge and require careful course management.

**Facilities:**

Appi Kogen Golf Club offers a comprehensive range of facilities to enhance your golfing experience. The clubhouse provides a comfortable and welcoming atmosphere, with spacious locker rooms, showers, and a well-stocked pro shop selling a wide selection of golf equipment, apparel, and accessories. The restaurant offers a variety of Japanese and Western dishes, with panoramic views of the course. Be sure to sample the local Iwate cuisine!

However, the true gem of Appi Kogen's facilities is its onsen (hot spring bath). After a challenging round of golf, nothing is more relaxing than soaking in the therapeutic waters of the onsen. The onsen features both indoor and outdoor baths, providing stunning views of the surrounding mountains. The natural minerals in the water are said to have healing properties, soothing tired muscles and promoting relaxation. The availability of a high-quality onsen elevates Appi Kogen Golf Club above many other courses in the region.

**Scenic Beauty:**

Appi Kogen is renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty, and the golf club is no exception. The course is surrounded by lush forests, rolling hills, and towering mountains, providing a stunning backdrop for your round. The best time to visit is during the autumn months (October-November), when the foliage transforms into a vibrant tapestry of colors. Spring (April-May) is also a beautiful time to visit, with the cherry blossoms in full bloom. However, the peak summer months offer pleasant temperatures and lush greenery. Regardless of the season, the scenic beauty of Appi Kogen will undoubtedly enhance your golfing experience. Even on a cloudy day, the mystical atmosphere adds a unique charm to the course.

**Access:**

Appi Kogen Golf Club is conveniently located within the Appi Kogen Resort area, making it easily accessible by both car and public transportation.

*   **By Car:** From Morioka City (the nearest major city), take the Tohoku Expressway north to the Appi Kogen Interchange (IC). From the IC, follow the signs to Appi Kogen Resort. The golf club is located within the resort area. The drive from Morioka takes approximately 50 minutes.

*   **By Train:** Take the JR Tohoku Shinkansen (bullet train) to Morioka Station. From Morioka Station, transfer to the JR Hanawa Line and take it to Appi Kogen Station. From Appi Kogen Station, take a taxi or the resort shuttle bus to the golf club. The train journey takes approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes. The resort shuttle bus is free for guests staying at Appi Kogen hotels.
