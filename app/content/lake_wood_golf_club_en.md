**Price & Booking:**

Playing at Lake Wood Golf Club is an investment in a premium golfing experience, and the price reflects this. Weekday green fees typically range from ¥25,000 to ¥35,000, while weekend and holiday rates can climb to ¥40,000 to ¥55,000. These prices usually include caddie fees (mandatory) and consumption tax, but it's always wise to confirm the breakdown during booking. Seasonal variations can significantly impact pricing. Spring (March-May) and autumn (September-November) are peak seasons, commanding higher rates due to the pleasant weather and stunning foliage. Booking well in advance, particularly during these periods, is highly recommended. Conversely, winter (December-February) offers discounted rates, although the weather can be unpredictable. Summer (June-August) can be hot and humid, but green fees may be lower, and early morning tee times are recommended to avoid the midday heat.

To secure your tee time, online booking through the official Lake Wood Golf Club website or reputable partnered platforms is the easiest and most reliable method. Phone reservations are also possible, but may require Japanese language proficiency. Many Japanese golf booking websites also offer packages that include accommodation at nearby hotels, making it a convenient 'Stay & Play' option. Consider booking through a travel agent specializing in golf tours in Japan for potential discounts and a hassle-free experience. Remember to inquire about any ongoing promotions or seasonal specials.

**Course Highlights:**

The course at Lake Wood is a testament to strategic design, demanding both precision and thoughtful decision-making. While the specific architect remains a debated topic, the course reflects a blend of classic and modern design principles, evident in its undulating fairways, strategically placed bunkers, and challenging green complexes. Water hazards come into play on several holes, adding to the complexity and requiring careful club selection.

While each hole presents its unique challenge, several stand out as signature holes. The par-3 7th hole, with its elevated tee box overlooking a picturesque pond and guarded by bunkers, demands accuracy and a steady nerve. The approach shot to the green requires precise distance control to avoid finding the water or sand. Another memorable hole is the par-5 14th, a dogleg right that rewards aggressive play off the tee but punishes wayward shots. Navigating the fairway bunkers and water hazard is crucial to setting up an eagle or birdie opportunity. The greens are typically well-maintained and fast, requiring a deft touch with the putter. The course's overall layout encourages strategic play, rewarding players who can think their way around the course rather than simply relying on brute force. The variety of tee boxes caters to golfers of all skill levels, ensuring an enjoyable experience for everyone.

**Access:**

Lake Wood Golf Club is conveniently located within easy reach of major transportation hubs. From Tokyo, the most convenient option is to take the JR Tokaido Line to Oiso Station. The journey takes approximately 1 hour and 15 minutes. From Oiso Station, a taxi ride to Lake Wood Golf Club takes approximately 15-20 minutes. Alternatively, the club offers a shuttle service from Oiso Station, but advance booking is essential.

For those driving, the course is accessible via the Odawara-Atsugi Road. Exit at the Oiso Interchange and follow the signs to Lake Wood Golf Club. The drive from Tokyo takes approximately 1 hour, depending on traffic conditions. Ample parking is available at the clubhouse.
