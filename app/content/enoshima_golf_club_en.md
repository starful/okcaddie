**Access: Reaching Your Coastal Golfing Destination**

Enoshima Golf Club is conveniently located in Fujisawa, Kanagawa, making it easily accessible from Tokyo and other major cities in the region.

*   **From Tokyo:** The most convenient way to reach Enoshima Golf Club from Tokyo is by train. Take the JR Tokaido Line to Fujisawa Station. From Fujisawa Station, you can take a taxi to the golf club, which takes approximately 15-20 minutes. Alternatively, you can take the Enoshima Electric Railway (Enoden) to Enoshima Station and then take a taxi to the club.

*   **By Car:** If you prefer to drive, Enoshima Golf Club is easily accessible via the Tomei Expressway and the Odawara-Atsugi Road. The drive from Tokyo takes approximately 1.5 to 2 hours, depending on traffic conditions. Parking is available at the golf club.
