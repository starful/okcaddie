**Introduction:**

PGM Golf Resort Okinawa, nestled in the heart of Onna, Okinawa, is more than just a golf course; it's an experience. This resort seamlessly blends the challenge of championship golf with the serene beauty of the Okinawan landscape. Designed to challenge seasoned players while remaining accessible to beginners, it offers a rewarding experience for all skill levels. Its well-maintained fairways, strategic bunkering, and breathtaking ocean views combine to create a truly unforgettable round.

**Price & Booking:**

Green fees at PGM Golf Resort Okinawa generally range from ¥10,000 to ¥18,000 on weekdays and ¥14,000 to ¥25,000 on weekends and holidays. Prices fluctuate depending on the season, with peak season (spring and autumn) commanding higher rates. Twilight rates are also available, offering a more affordable option for late afternoon rounds. Booking is highly recommended, especially during peak season. Popular booking platforms include the PGM official website, Rakuten GORA, and Japan Golf Network. Booking through these platforms often unlocks special packages and discounts. For the best value, consider golfing during the off-season (summer or winter), where you can often find significantly lower green fees and enjoy a less crowded course.

**Course Accessibility:**

PGM Golf Resort Okinawa operates as a public resort, welcoming both members and non-members. While there are membership options available, visitors are easily able to book tee times and experience the course. This accessibility makes it a popular choice for tourists looking to incorporate golf into their Okinawan vacation. The ease of booking and the welcoming atmosphere contribute to its appeal as a top golfing destination in Okinawa.

**Course Highlights:**

The course at PGM Golf Resort Okinawa features a strategic layout that rewards thoughtful play. While the specific architect remains less publicized, the course's design philosophy emphasizes precision and course management over brute power. Water hazards come into play on several holes, demanding accurate tee shots and approaches. The bunkering is strategically placed to penalize errant shots and protect the greens.

Signature holes include the par-3 17th, which boasts a stunning ocean view and requires a precise shot over a ravine to a well-protected green. The par-5 18th provides a challenging finishing hole, demanding a long and accurate drive followed by a strategic approach to avoid the strategically placed bunkers guarding the green. The varied terrain and well-manicured conditions make every hole a unique and enjoyable challenge.

**Facilities:**

The clubhouse at PGM Golf Resort Okinawa is well-appointed and provides a comfortable space to relax before and after your round. The restaurant offers a variety of Japanese and international cuisine, with many dishes featuring fresh, local ingredients. The pro shop is stocked with a wide selection of golf equipment, apparel, and souvenirs.

However, the true highlight of the facilities is the Onsen (hot spring bath). After a long day on the course, there's nothing quite like soaking in the rejuvenating waters of the Onsen. The Onsen features both indoor and outdoor baths, offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape. The water is rich in minerals, providing therapeutic benefits for tired muscles. This exceptional Onsen experience elevates PGM Golf Resort Okinawa above many other courses in the region. Locker rooms are spacious and well-maintained, with all the amenities you would expect from a top-tier resort.

**Scenic Beauty:**

PGM Golf Resort Okinawa is blessed with stunning natural beauty. The best time to visit is during the spring (March-May) or autumn (September-November) when the weather is mild and pleasant. The course is surrounded by lush tropical vegetation, including vibrant hibiscus flowers and towering palm trees. The ocean views from several holes are simply breathtaking, adding to the overall appeal of the course. The meticulously manicured fairways and greens contrast beautifully with the natural surroundings, creating a visually stunning golfing experience. Keep an eye out for local wildlife, including colorful birds and lizards, adding to the natural charm of the resort.

**Access:**

PGM Golf Resort Okinawa is conveniently located in Onna, Okinawa, approximately a 60-minute drive from Naha Airport (OKA). The easiest way to access the resort is by rental car. From Naha Airport, take the Okinawa Expressway (toll road) north and exit at the Ishikawa IC. From there, follow the signs to PGM Golf Resort Okinawa. Alternatively, you can take a taxi from the airport, but this can be a more expensive option. Limousine buses also operate from the airport to various hotels in Onna, from which you can take a short taxi ride to the resort. The resort provides ample parking for guests. GPS navigation is highly recommended, especially for those unfamiliar with the area.

**Final Verdict:**

PGM Golf Resort Okinawa is an excellent choice for golfers seeking a combination of challenging play, stunning scenery, and comfortable accommodations. Its accessible public status, coupled with its well-maintained course and excellent facilities, make it a must-play destination in Okinawa. The onsen is a particular highlight, providing a unique and relaxing experience after a round of golf. While not the cheapest option on the island, the value for money is undeniable, especially considering the overall quality of the resort. I highly recommend PGM Golf Resort Okinawa to any traveler looking for a memorable golfing experience in paradise.
