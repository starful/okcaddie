Beppu Golf Club falls into the premium category, reflecting the quality of the course and its exclusive nature. Expect to pay in the range of JPY 35,000 to JPY 50,000 per round on weekdays, and JPY 45,000 to JPY 60,000 on weekends and holidays. These prices typically include green fees, caddie fees (highly recommended, if not mandatory), and consumption tax. Prices can fluctuate depending on the season and any ongoing promotions.

**Scenic Beauty:**

Beppu Golf Club is blessed with stunning natural beauty. The course is situated on a hillside overlooking Beppu Bay, offering panoramic views of the coastline and the surrounding mountains. The best time to visit is during the spring (March-May) when the cherry blossoms are in full bloom, or during the autumn (September-November) when the fall foliage paints the landscape in vibrant colors. The course is beautifully landscaped, with a variety of trees, shrubs, and flowers that add to its visual appeal. The fresh sea air and the tranquil atmosphere create a truly relaxing and enjoyable golfing experience. You can often see steam rising in the distance from the various onsen in Beppu.

**Access:**

Getting to Beppu Golf Club requires careful planning.

*   *From Fukuoka Airport (FUK):* The most convenient option is to take the Sonic Limited Express train from Hakata Station (Fukuoka) to Beppu Station. The journey takes approximately 2 hours. From Beppu Station, take a taxi to Beppu Golf Club, which is about a 30-40 minute drive.

*   *From Oita Airport (OIT):* Take a bus from Oita Airport to Beppu Station. The journey takes approximately 45 minutes. From Beppu Station, take a taxi to Beppu Golf Club.

*   *By Car:* If you are driving, take the Oita Expressway to the Kitsuki Interchange. Follow the signs to Beppu Golf Club.

It's advisable to arrange transportation in advance, especially if you are not familiar with the area. Consider hiring a private car or using a taxi service.
