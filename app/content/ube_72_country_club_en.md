**Course Highlights:**

Ube 72 Country Club boasts four distinct 18-hole courses, each with its own unique character and challenges:

*   *Shin-Kurume Course:* Known for its strategic layout and undulating fairways, Shin-Kurume is a visually stunning course that requires precision and thoughtful club selection. The course is framed by mature trees and strategically placed bunkers, demanding accuracy from tee to green. Signature holes often feature water hazards that come into play, adding to the excitement and challenge.
*   *Ogochi Course:* A challenging course with dramatic elevation changes, Ogochi rewards bold play and offers breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding landscape. Expect demanding tee shots and approaches that require careful consideration of wind conditions. The signature hole is likely to be a par-3 played from an elevated tee, showcasing the course's dramatic topography.
*   *Enzan Course:* Considered the most accessible of the four courses, Enzan is a more forgiving layout that is ideal for golfers of all skill levels. However, don't be fooled by its apparent ease; strategically placed hazards and subtle green contours will still test your skills. The course is characterized by its wide fairways and well-manicured greens.
*   *Haraki Course:* This course offers a blend of challenge and playability. It has a good mix of holes that will test your accuracy and distance control. The Haraki course often hosts local tournaments due to its versatility.

While the specific architects for each course are not widely publicized, they are designed to provide a balance of strategic challenge and visual appeal. Each course is meticulously maintained, showcasing the club's commitment to providing a world-class golfing experience.
