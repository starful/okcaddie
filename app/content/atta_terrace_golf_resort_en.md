**Introduction:**

Atta Terrace Golf Resort is more than just a golf course; it's an immersion into the refined elegance and natural splendor of Okinawa. Nestled in the lush hills of Onna, this meticulously crafted course offers a golfing experience that seamlessly blends challenge and tranquility. It's a destination where the precision of the game meets the serenity of island life, appealing to seasoned golfers seeking a premium escape. The prestige of Atta Terrace lies in its unwavering commitment to quality, from the immaculate course conditions to the impeccable service that anticipates every need. This is a resort where golf is elevated to an art form, and every round becomes a cherished memory.

**Price & Booking:**

Playing at Atta Terrace Golf Resort is an investment in an unforgettable experience. Expect green fees to range from ¥25,000 to ¥40,000 on weekdays and ¥35,000 to ¥55,000 on weekends and holidays. Prices fluctuate depending on the season, with peak rates during the spring (March-May) and autumn (September-November) months when the weather is most pleasant. Twilight rates are often available for those who prefer to play in the late afternoon, offering a slightly more affordable option. Booking in advance is highly recommended, especially during peak season. The easiest way to secure a tee time is through the Atta Terrace official website, where you can also explore stay & play packages that bundle accommodation and golf. Alternatively, affiliated travel agencies specializing in Japanese golf tours can handle your booking and arrange transportation. Keep an eye out for seasonal promotions and special packages that may offer discounted rates or additional perks.

**Course Accessibility:**

Atta Terrace Golf Resort operates as a semi-private facility. While open to the public, priority is given to members and guests staying at the affiliated Atta Terrace Club Towers and The Atta Terrace Beach Villas. This policy ensures a consistently high level of service and course maintenance for all players. Non-members can typically book tee times, but it's advisable to do so well in advance, particularly during busy periods. This carefully managed accessibility adds to the exclusivity and premium feel of the resort.

**Course Highlights:**

The Atta Terrace golf course is a testament to strategic design and meticulous landscaping. While details about the specific architect are often less emphasized than the overall experience, the layout speaks volumes about the thoughtful planning that went into its creation. The course winds its way through rolling hills and lush vegetation, offering a variety of challenges for golfers of all skill levels.

While specific "signature holes" are not officially designated, several stand out for their unique characteristics and stunning views:

*   **The Par-3 holes:** The Par-3s offer breathtaking panoramic views of the East China Sea. Precise club selection is crucial, as the wind can play a significant role in determining the ball's trajectory.
*   **The strategic doglegs:** These holes demand careful planning and accurate tee shots. Players must consider the placement of bunkers and water hazards to avoid trouble and set up an optimal approach to the green.
*   **The undulating greens:** The greens at Atta Terrace are known for their subtle breaks and challenging speeds. Putting here requires a keen eye and a delicate touch.

The course rewards strategic thinking and precise execution, making it a satisfying challenge for experienced golfers. At the same time, the forgiving fairways and multiple tee boxes allow less experienced players to enjoy the game without feeling overwhelmed. The immaculate course conditions, meticulously manicured greens, and strategically placed bunkers contribute to the overall premium golfing experience.

**Facilities:**

Atta Terrace Golf Resort boasts a range of world-class facilities designed to enhance the golfing experience. The clubhouse is a modern and elegant structure that seamlessly blends into the surrounding landscape. Inside, you'll find a well-stocked pro shop offering a wide selection of golf apparel, equipment, and accessories. The friendly and knowledgeable staff are always on hand to provide assistance and advice.

The resort's restaurant offers a delectable array of local and international cuisine, prepared with fresh, seasonal ingredients. Whether you're looking for a casual lunch after your round or a fine-dining experience in the evening, the restaurant caters to all tastes.

However, the true highlight of the facilities is the exquisite Onsen (hot spring bath). After a challenging round of golf, there's no better way to relax and rejuvenate than soaking in the therapeutic waters of the Onsen. The Onsen features both indoor and outdoor baths, offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape. The mineral-rich water is believed to have numerous health benefits, including relieving muscle soreness and improving circulation. The meticulous cleanliness and serene atmosphere of the Onsen create a truly unforgettable experience.

**Access:**

Getting to Atta Terrace Golf Resort is relatively straightforward, although a rental car is highly recommended for exploring the surrounding area.

*   **From Naha Airport (OKA):** The most common entry point to Okinawa. The drive to Atta Terrace Golf Resort takes approximately 60-90 minutes by car, depending on traffic conditions. Take the Okinawa Expressway (toll road) towards Nago, and exit at the Ishikawa Interchange. From there, follow the signs to Atta Terrace.
*   **By Limousine Bus:** Some limousine bus services operate from Naha Airport to major hotels in the Onna area. Check with your hotel or the airport information desk for schedules and availability.
*   **By Taxi:** Taxis are readily available at Naha Airport, but the fare to Atta Terrace will be quite expensive (expect to pay around ¥15,000-¥20,000).

Once you arrive at the resort, parking is readily available and conveniently located near the clubhouse.
