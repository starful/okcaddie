## Course Overview

Fukuoka Kokusai Golf presents an 18-hole, par 72 championship layout nestled within the forested hills of Munakata, Fukuoka. The course typically plays between 6,800 and 7,100 yards from the back tees, offering a robust test for all skill levels. Fairways are predominantly Zoysia, known for its firm playability, while the greens feature quick, consistent Bentgrass.

Its defining characteristic is the dense, mature forest that frames almost every hole, demanding accuracy over raw power. Strategic bunkering and subtle elevation changes are integral to the design, ensuring that each shot requires careful consideration.

## Layout & Strategy

The course challenges golfers with a series of tight tee shots and strategic approaches. Consider the par-4 5th hole, which demands a precise drive through a narrow corridor of trees to avoid trouble on both sides. A 3-wood or hybrid off the tee often provides the best angle for an approach to a green guarded by bunkers.

The par-3 14th hole typically plays downhill, requiring a mid-iron to a green that slopes subtly from back to front. Misjudging the distance can lead to a long, uphill two-putt or a chip from collection areas. Finally, the dogleg-left par-5 17th presents a classic risk-reward scenario. An aggressive line over the left-side forest with a driver can shorten the hole significantly, but the safer play is a faded 2-iron or hybrid to the center of the fairway, leaving a clear second shot and a manageable third into a tiered green.

## Practical Info

Green fees at Fukuoka Kokusai Golf generally range from approximately JPY 8,000-14,000 on weekdays and JPY 12,000-20,000 on weekends and holidays, usually including a cart. Reservations are highly recommended, particularly for weekend play, and can typically be made 30-60 days in advance.

Golf carts are standard, often equipped with GPS, and are usually mandatory. While walking is less common, caddies are available upon request for an additional fee. The dress code adheres to standard golf club etiquette: collared shirts, tailored trousers or shorts, and soft spikes are required.

## Access

Fukuoka Kokusai Golf is conveniently located in Munakata, Fukuoka. The course is approximately a 45-60 minute drive north from Fukuoka City's Hakata Station or Tenjin district.

For those traveling by train, the nearest major stations on the JR Kagoshima Main Line, such as Fukuma or Akama, are within a 20-30 minute taxi ride from the course. Ample free parking is available on-site for those arriving by private vehicle.

## Clubhouse Notes

The clubhouse offers well-maintained locker rooms and a spacious communal bath (ofuro), providing a relaxing post-round experience typical of Japanese golf clubs. This is a perfect way to soothe tired muscles before departing. The restaurant serves a range of Japanese and Western cuisine, with local specialties such as fresh seafood dishes, reflecting Munakata's coastal proximity, often featured on the menu.

## Tips

The ideal months to play Fukuoka Kokusai Golf are April-May and October-November, when the weather is mild and the course conditions are at their prime. During summer, expect high humidity, while winter can bring chilly temperatures. A common mistake here is underestimating the narrowness of the fairways; prioritize accuracy over distance off the tee.

Booking well in advance, especially for weekend tee times, is crucial as peak season demand can quickly fill slots, often leading to higher green fees. Be prepared for varied green breaks, as the putting surfaces often have subtle undulations that defy initial reads.
