**Access:**

Reaching Sodegaura Country Club requires careful planning, as it is primarily accessed by car. From central Tokyo, the journey typically takes around 1.5 to 2 hours, depending on traffic conditions. The most convenient route is via the Tokyo Bay Aqua-Line and the Tateyama Expressway. Detailed directions can be provided by the club upon confirmation of a tee time through a member. Alternatively, you can take a train to Sodegaura Station on the JR Uchibo Line. From Sodegaura Station, a taxi ride to the club will take approximately 15-20 minutes. It is advisable to arrange for a taxi in advance, especially during peak seasons. While public transportation is an option, it is generally less convenient than driving, particularly if you are carrying golf clubs. The club provides ample parking for members and guests.
