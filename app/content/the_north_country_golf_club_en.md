The North Country Golf Club in Chitose is one of the **easiest Hokkaido rounds to logistics-plan**—close to New Chitose Airport (CTS), generally visitor-bookable in season, and popular with golfers connecting Sapporo trips.

## Quick facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Location** | Chitose, Hokkaido |
| **Style** | Open / links-leaning parkland feel |
| **Visitor access** | Strong portal availability in summer |
| **Airport** | New Chitose (CTS) nearby |
| **Best months** | June–September (shoulder can be cold/windy) |
| **Best for** | Arrival-day or departure-day golf, mixed groups |

## Who it's for

- Travelers who land at **CTS** and want golf without a long inland drive
- Groups building a Hokkaido loop with [Hokkaido Classic](/course/hokkaido_classic_golf_club_en) or Sapporo-area courses
- Mid-handicappers who can keep the ball in play when wind rises

Not a winter destination—Hokkaido courses shut or are impractical for most visitors in deep snow months. See [Hokkaido summer golf](/guide/hokkaido-summer-golf).

## Course overview

Open corridors and wind matter more than jungle rough. Keep trajectory controlled; club up into headwinds. Bunkers catch greedy lines. Greens can be firm in dry summer stretches—land approaches on the correct portion rather than spinning every wedge to a stop.

Summer daylight is long—still book sensible tee times if you have evening flights.

## Green fees & booking

Hokkaido peak summer is demand season. **Planning ranges**:

- **Weekday**: often mid-¥10,000s to low-¥20,000s
- **Weekend / holiday weeks**: higher; Obon and consecutive holidays fill fast

Book: [North Country booking](/booking/the_north_country_golf_club_en). Add rentals early during July–August.

## Access

- **Airport**: CTS → often about **20–40 minutes** by car depending on exact routing and traffic
- Ideal for **fly-play-fly** or play-then-train to Sapporo
- Rental car or pre-booked taxi; don’t rely on crowded airport buses with hard cases

If comparing Chitose/Eniwa options, also look at [Eniwa Country Club](/course/eniwa_country_club_en).

## Dress code & tips

- Soft spikes; layered clothing even in July mornings
- Insect repellent can help in peak summer near vegetation
- Related: [Sapporo Wattsu](/course/sapporo_golf_club_wattsu_course_en) · [Booking tips](/guide/booking-tips-japan)

## Bottom line

North Country is the **CTS-convenience Hokkaido pick**. Book summer tee times early, watch wind, and use the airport proximity as the real advantage.
