**Price & Booking:**

Green fees at Katayamazu Golf Club typically range from ¥25,000 to ¥45,000 on weekdays and ¥35,000 to ¥60,000 on weekends and holidays. Prices can fluctuate depending on the season, time of day, and whether you are a member's guest. Staying at the adjacent Katayamazu Onsen hotels often unlocks package deals that include green fees and accommodation, offering significant savings. Booking well in advance is crucial, especially during peak seasons like spring (cherry blossom season) and autumn (fall foliage). Consider booking 2-3 months ahead to secure your preferred tee time. Weekday play often offers better value and less crowded conditions. Always confirm the final price and any associated caddy fees or cart rentals when booking. Check their official website or contact the club directly for the most up-to-date pricing information. Early bird specials and twilight rates might also be available, especially during the shoulder seasons.

**Course Highlights:**

Katayamazu Golf Club boasts a strategic layout designed to challenge golfers of all skill levels. While the architect's name isn't widely publicized, the course's design reflects a deep understanding of strategic golf course architecture. The course meanders through gently rolling hills, offering stunning views of Lake Shibayama and the surrounding landscape. The fairways are meticulously maintained, providing excellent lies. The greens are known for their undulating surfaces and true roll. Water hazards come into play on several holes, demanding accurate shot placement.

One of the signature holes is arguably the par-3 16th, which requires a precise tee shot over water to a well-guarded green with the lake as a stunning backdrop. The par-5 holes offer opportunities for scoring, but also demand strategic decision-making off the tee. The bunkering is strategically placed to penalize errant shots and add to the challenge. The course is designed to reward thoughtful play and penalize overly aggressive approaches. The layout encourages golfers to use all the clubs in their bag and requires a blend of power and precision. The constant presence of the lake and the sea breeze adds another layer of complexity to the game.

**Access:**

Katayamazu Golf Club is located in Kaga, Ishikawa Prefecture. The most convenient way to access the club is via Komatsu Airport (KMQ), which is served by domestic flights from major cities like Tokyo and Osaka. From Komatsu Airport, take a taxi (approximately 30 minutes) or a bus (approximately 45 minutes) to Katayamazu Onsen. From Katayamazu Onsen, the golf club is a short taxi ride away.

Alternatively, you can take the Hokuriku Shinkansen (bullet train) to Kaga Onsen Station. From Kaga Onsen Station, take a taxi (approximately 15 minutes) to Katayamazu Golf Club. Rental cars are also available at both Komatsu Airport and Kaga Onsen Station, offering greater flexibility for exploring the surrounding area. Driving directions can be easily obtained using online mapping services. Parking is available at the club. Consider the train for a relaxing and scenic journey through the Japanese countryside.
