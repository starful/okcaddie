## Who it's for

- Players who want a **tournament-tested layout** rather than pure resort width

If your only goal is easy scoring photos, a softer resort nine may fit better. MCC asks for iron control and green patience.

## Course overview

Expect tree-lined corridors, strategic bunkers, and greens that punish long-iron approaches left above the hole. Ocean climate means humidity and occasional crosswinds. Play to safe sides of pins; bent surfaces can look receptive and still release.

Clubhouse service follows Japanese championship-club standards. Confirm whether your product is self-play or caddie-assisted—tournament weeks change staffing and available tee windows.

## Green fees & booking

Championship-city rates vary. **Planning ranges**:

- **Weekday visitor packages**: often mid-¥10,000s to low-¥20,000s
- **Weekend / peak winter golf season**: frequently ¥20,000s territory with lunch

**Book via** [Miyazaki Country Club booking](/booking/miyazaki_country_club_en). Avoid assuming tee times during JLPGA championship weeks—blockouts are real. Cross-check neighboring options in our [Miyazaki pro tour courses](/guide/miyazaki-pro-tour-courses) guide.

## Access

- **Airport**: Miyazaki (KMI) → typically **20–40 minutes** by car depending on hotel base
- Rental car is simplest for multi-course Miyazaki trips ([Tom Watson](/course/tom_watson_golf_course_en), Phoenix, MCC)
- Taxi from city hotels works for a single morning tee

Mild winters make Miyazaki a popular escape from snow-country golfers—book lodging early for January–February weekends.
