**Introduction:**

This exclusive private club, located in Iruma, Saitama Prefecture, is a haven for discerning golfers seeking an unparalleled experience. Unlike many courses designed for speed and accessibility, Sayama prioritizes strategic play, pristine conditions, and a tranquil atmosphere. The club's commitment to tradition and excellence makes it a truly special destination, albeit one that requires considerable effort to access. The prestige associated with playing Sayama is well-earned, stemming from its challenging layout, impeccable service, and unwavering commitment to the highest standards of golfing excellence. A round at Sayama is more than just a game; it's an immersion into the refined world of Japanese golf at its very best.

**Price & Booking:**

As a highly exclusive private club, Sayama Golf Club does not publicly advertise its green fees. However, estimates for guests introduced by members typically range from ¥30,000 to ¥50,000 per round on weekdays, and potentially higher on weekends and holidays. These fees often include caddie fees, green fees, and consumption tax. Booking is strictly limited to members or those introduced through affiliated golf clubs. It is also worth noting that caddie service is mandatory, further contributing to the overall cost of play.

**Access:**

Reaching Sayama Golf Club requires careful planning, especially if you are not familiar with the area. The nearest major city is Tokyo.

*   **By Train:** From Tokyo Station or Shinjuku Station, take the JR Saikyo Line or Shonan-Shinjuku Line to Kawagoe Station. From Kawagoe Station, take a taxi to Sayama Golf Club (approximately 30-40 minutes).
*   **By Car:** From central Tokyo, take the Kan-etsu Expressway to the Kawagoe IC. From the Kawagoe IC, follow Route 16 towards Iruma. The golf club is located off Route 16.

It is highly recommended to arrange transportation in advance, especially if you do not speak Japanese. Taxis are readily available at Kawagoe Station, but it is helpful to have the golf club's address and phone number written down to show the driver.
