**Access:**

Reaching Fuchu Country Club requires careful planning, as it is located in a relatively suburban area. The most convenient way to access the club is by car or taxi. From central Tokyo, the journey typically takes around 45 minutes to an hour, depending on traffic conditions. Alternatively, you can take a train to Fuchu Honmachi Station on the JR Musashino Line, followed by a short taxi ride to the club. The club offers ample parking for members and their guests.
