## Course Overview
Matsuyama Seaside Golf presents 18 holes of links-style golf, playing to a par of 72. The course typically measures between 6,600 and 7,000 yards from the back tees, offering a fair challenge without excessive length. The original design team focused on integrating the layout with its natural coastal environment, ensuring panoramic ocean views from many points.

Established with an emphasis on its unique seaside location, fairways often feature a resilient Zoysia blend, while greens are predominantly Bentgrass for consistent putting surfaces. Its signature characteristics include exposed fairways, strategic bunkering that mimics natural dunes, and the constant presence of the Seto Inland Sea's visual influence and prevailing winds.

## Layout & Strategy
Hole 3, a challenging par 4, demands a precise tee shot over a subtle undulation, with the ocean subtly guarding the entire right side. Players should consider a conservative 3-wood or hybrid to avoid a penalizing crosswind that can push drives towards the out-of-bounds. The approach shot often plays longer than its yardage due to an elevated green and a tendency for the wind to knock down shots.

The scenic par 14, a mid-length par 3, frequently plays directly into or across the prevailing wind. It requires a confident carry over a natural depression to a green that slopes gently from back to front. Any shot leaking right can find a collection area, making an up-and-down save difficult.

## Practical Info
Green fees generally range from ¥9,000-¥15,000 on weekdays, with weekend and holiday play typically between ¥14,000-¥22,000, including cart fees. Matsuyama Seaside Golf is a public course, though advance reservations are strongly recommended, especially during peak seasons.

Riding carts are standard and often equipped with GPS, allowing for efficient self-play. Standard golf attire is expected: collared shirts, tailored trousers or shorts, and soft spike golf shoes. While self-play is common, caddies are available upon request; inquire about availability and associated fees when booking.

## Access
Matsuyama Seaside Golf is conveniently located within Matsuyama City, Ehime Prefecture. The primary rail access point is JR Matsuyama Station.

From JR Matsuyama Station, the course is approximately a 35-45 minute drive, covering a distance of about 15-20 kilometers, depending on traffic. Ample on-site parking is available for personal vehicles.

## Clubhouse Notes
The clubhouse offers well-maintained locker rooms with functional changing facilities for pre and post-round use. A communal bath (ofuro) is available, providing a relaxing soak typical of Japanese golf facilities after your game. The clubhouse restaurant serves a variety of regional specialties, frequently featuring local seafood preparations or dishes highlighting Ehime's famous mikan citrus.

## Tips
Optimal playing conditions are typically found in spring (April-May) and autumn (October-November), offering mild temperatures and clearer skies. Common golfer mistakes include underestimating the impact of prevailing coastal winds on ball flight and misjudging distances, especially on approach shots to elevated or exposed greens.

Be prepared for sudden shifts in weather; coastal courses can experience rapid changes in wind strength or short rain showers, so pack appropriate rain gear. Expect higher green fees and more crowded tee sheets during major Japanese holidays such as Golden Week and Obon.
