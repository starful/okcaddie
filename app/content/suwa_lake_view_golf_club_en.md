More than just a round of golf, it's an immersion into the stunning natural beauty of central Japan. While not boasting the extravagant luxury of some of Japan's more exclusive clubs, Suwa Lake View delivers exceptional value for money, making it accessible to a wider range of golfers seeking a memorable day on the links. Its charm lies in its unpretentious atmosphere, coupled with the sheer majesty of its location. It's a place where you can disconnect from the everyday and reconnect with the simple joy of the game, surrounded by unparalleled natural splendor.

Caddie fees are usually extra, ranging from ¥3,000 - ¥4,000 per player. Seasonal discounts are often available, particularly during the shoulder seasons (spring and autumn).

To secure your tee time, it's highly recommended to book in advance, especially during peak seasons (Golden Week in late April/early May, Obon week in mid-August, and autumn foliage season in October/November). You can book directly through the golf club's website (Japanese only, so use a translation tool), or through various online golf booking platforms popular in Japan. If you are staying at a hotel in the Suwa area, the concierge can often assist with booking and transportation arrangements. Booking a few weeks in advance is advisable to ensure you get your preferred tee time. Consider playing on a weekday for lower prices and fewer crowds.
