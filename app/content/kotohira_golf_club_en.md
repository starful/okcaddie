**Introduction:**

Kotohira Golf Club stands as a beacon of golfing excellence in the verdant landscape of Shikoku. This prestigious course, located near the iconic Kotohira-gu Shrine, offers a golfing experience that blends strategic play with breathtaking natural vistas. More than just a round of golf, it's an immersion into Japanese hospitality, where every detail is meticulously crafted to ensure an unforgettable visit. Kotohira Golf Club boasts a unique charm that sets it apart, appealing to seasoned players seeking a challenge and discerning travelers craving a tranquil escape. Its reputation for exceptional course conditions and personalized service makes it a must-play destination for anyone exploring the golfing delights of Japan.

**Price & Booking:**

Playing at Kotohira Golf Club is an investment in a premium golfing experience. Expect weekday green fees to range from ¥25,000 to ¥35,000, while weekend rates typically climb to ¥35,000 to ¥50,000. These prices generally include green fees, caddie fees (compulsory), and consumption tax. Seasonal variations can significantly impact pricing. Shoulder seasons (spring and autumn) often offer slightly lower rates compared to peak seasons (summer and public holidays). Booking in advance is highly recommended, particularly during popular travel periods. Tee times can be secured through the official Kotohira Golf Club website, or through partnered hotels and travel agencies that offer stay-and-play packages. These packages can represent excellent value, especially when combining accommodation, meals, and golf. Look out for early bird discounts and promotional offers, which are occasionally available during off-peak periods. Remember to inquire about cancellation policies before confirming your booking.

**Course Highlights:**

The Kotohira Golf Club course is a masterpiece of strategic design, blending seamlessly with the natural contours of the landscape. While the architect's name is not widely publicized, the design philosophy emphasizes precision and thoughtful shot-making. The layout features a variety of challenging holes, each requiring a different approach and club selection. The fairways are typically generous, but strategically placed bunkers and water hazards demand accuracy from the tee. The greens are known for their immaculate condition and subtle undulations, testing even the most skilled putters.

Signature holes include:

*   **The Par-3 7th:** A visually stunning hole that plays over a ravine to a well-protected green. Club selection is crucial, as wind conditions can significantly impact ball flight. The panoramic views from the tee box are simply breathtaking.
*   **The Par-4 12th:** A demanding dogleg right that requires a precise drive to avoid the strategically placed fairway bunker. The approach shot is equally challenging, with a tiered green guarded by a deep bunker on the left.
*   **The Par-5 18th:** A memorable finishing hole that offers the opportunity for a birdie or even an eagle. A well-placed drive sets up a long second shot to a green protected by a water hazard. The pressure is on as you finish your round in front of the clubhouse.

The course rewards intelligent play and penalizes errant shots. The overall design encourages strategic thinking and precise execution, making it a challenging and rewarding experience for golfers of all skill levels.

Kotohira Golf Club boasts a comprehensive range of facilities designed to enhance the overall golfing experience. **Access:**

Kotohira Golf Club is conveniently located near Kotohira, Kagawa Prefecture. The most convenient access point is Takamatsu Airport (TAK).

**From Takamatsu Airport (TAK):**

1.  **Rental Car:** The easiest way to reach the club is by renting a car at Takamatsu Airport. The drive takes approximately 60-75 minutes, covering a distance of roughly 50 kilometers. Follow the signs for the Takamatsu Expressway (高松自動車道). Take the Kotohira IC (琴平インターチェンジ) exit. From the Kotohira IC, follow the local roads to the Kotohira Golf Club. Navigation systems are highly recommended.
2.  **Train & Taxi:** Take the Kotoden Airport Limousine Bus from Takamatsu Airport to Takamatsu Station (approx. 45 minutes). From Takamatsu Station, take the Kotoden Kotohira Line (琴電琴平線) to Kotohira Station (approx. 60 minutes). From Kotohira Station, a taxi to the golf club takes approximately 15-20 minutes. This option requires more planning and coordination but is viable for those not comfortable driving.
