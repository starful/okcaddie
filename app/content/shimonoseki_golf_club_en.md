Shimonoseki Golf Club is a **prestigious private club** on the Kanmon Strait coast in Yamaguchi. Treat it as a member/guest destination first—not a walk-up resort round you “just book on GORA.”

## Quick facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Location** | Shimonoseki, Yamaguchi |
| **Style** | Seaside / championship private |
| **Turf** | Korai fairways, bent greens (typical) |
| **Visitor access** | Member guest / limited visitor products—confirm before travel |
| **Best months** | April–June, Oct–Nov |
| **Best for** | Golfers with a sponsor intro or flexible dates |

## Who it's for

- Players who already have a **member invitation** or travel agent who can clear guest rules
- Golfers who like **coastal wind** and firm conditions more than resort width
- Anyone researching “how to play Shimonoseki” who needs the **access reality** upfront

If you need a guaranteed public tee near Kanmon, look at easier bookable products in Kitakyushu / western Honshu instead of banking on this club alone.

## Course overview

The layout plays with **strait breeze** and strategic bunkering. Expect Korai lies that reward crisp iron contact and bent greens that run true when dry. Wind is the main “extra club”—especially on exposed approaches. Keep drives in the short grass; recovery from thick coastal rough eats strokes.

Pace and hospitality follow classic Japanese private-club norms: punctuality, dress, and locker-room flow matter. Don’t plan a rushed photo round between sightseeing stops.

## Green fees & booking

Private-club guest fees and available products vary widely. As a **planning range** only (not a quote):

- Guest packages often land in the **¥20,000s+** band once lunch, cart, and taxes stack—sometimes higher on weekends
- Member-accompanied rates and open visitor windows (if any) are different products

**How to proceed**

1. Check [booking](/booking/shimonoseki_golf_club_en) for any live visitor listing
2. If nothing shows, use a hotel concierge / Japan golf desk and ask about **guest introduction** requirements
3. Confirm handicap expectations, dress, and caddie rules in writing before you fly

Do not assume same-day availability. Prestigious Kanmon clubs fill with members first.

## Access

- **Airports**: Kitakyushu (KKJ) or Fukuoka (FUK) + car; Shin-Shimonoseki / Shimonoseki stations for rail travelers who then transfer by taxi
- **Drive**: build **60–90+ minutes** from FUK depending on traffic; KKJ is often closer
- Golf bags on crowded local buses are a poor plan—taxi or rental car

Pair with Kanmon Bridge sightseeing only after the round; morning tees need an early start from Hakata hotels.

## Dress code & tips

- Full private-club dress: collared shirt, tailored trousers/shorts, soft spikes
- Wind layer and rain shell in the bag year-round
