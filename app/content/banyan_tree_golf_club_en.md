**Price & Booking:**

Playing at Banyan Tree Golf Club requires a significant investment, reflecting the quality and exclusivity of the experience. Weekday green fees typically range from JPY 40,000 to JPY 60,000, while weekend and holiday rates can climb to JPY 70,000 to JPY 90,000 or even higher depending on the season and any ongoing promotions. These prices usually include green fees, caddie fees (mandatory), and consumption tax. Cart fees are often extra.

Seasonal Tip: The shoulder seasons (spring - April/May and autumn - October/November) offer the most pleasant weather for golfing in the Tokyo area. The course is particularly stunning during the autumn foliage season when the surrounding trees burst into vibrant colors. Booking well in advance, especially during these peak seasons, is highly recommended to secure your preferred tee time. Package deals that include accommodation at a nearby Banyan Tree hotel or resort can offer significant value and enhance the overall experience. Furthermore, keep an eye out for special promotions or corporate packages that may be available through the Banyan Tree Hotels & Resorts website or affiliated travel agencies. Online booking, while sometimes available, is best handled through direct contact to ensure personalized service and confirmation of all details.

**Access:**

Reaching Banyan Tree Golf Club requires careful planning, but the journey is well worth the effort. The most convenient way to access the club is typically by private car or taxi. From central Tokyo, the drive typically takes around 1 to 1.5 hours, depending on traffic conditions. Detailed driving directions can be obtained from the club's website or concierge.

Alternatively, you can take a train to the nearest major station, such as Fussa Station (福生駅) or Haijima Station (拝島駅), and then take a taxi to the golf club. The taxi ride from either station is approximately 15-20 minutes. Pre-arranging a taxi is recommended, especially during peak seasons. Some Banyan Tree properties may offer shuttle services to and from the golf club for their guests. It is advisable to inquire about this option when making your accommodation reservations. While public transportation is an option, it is generally less convenient and more time-consuming than driving or taking a taxi. Proper planning and clear directions are essential to ensure a smooth and stress-free journey to this golfing oasis.
