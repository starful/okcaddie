**Introduction:**

Kagoshima Airport 36 Country Club presents a unique proposition in Japanese golf: accessible luxury. Often overlooked by international travelers in favor of courses closer to major city centers, this gem in Kirishima offers an outstanding golfing experience without the exorbitant price tag associated with more prestigious clubs. Its proximity to Kagoshima Airport (KOJ) is a major draw, allowing for a seamless transition from plane to tee box. The club boasts two distinct 18-hole courses, each presenting unique challenges and scenic vistas, ensuring a varied and rewarding experience for every golfer. More than just convenience, Kagoshima Airport 36 CC offers a taste of authentic Kyushu hospitality and natural beauty.

**Price & Booking:**

Green fees at Kagoshima Airport 36 CC are comparatively reasonable, especially considering the quality of the courses and facilities. Weekday rates generally range from ¥8,000 to ¥12,000 per person, including caddie fees (which are often mandatory). Weekend and holiday rates can increase to ¥15,000 to ¥20,000. Prices fluctuate depending on the season, with the shoulder seasons (spring and autumn) offering the best value.

Booking is relatively easy. The club partners with several online booking platforms specializing in Japanese golf courses, and many local hotels offer stay-and-play packages that include guaranteed tee times. Advance booking is highly recommended, particularly during peak seasons and on weekends, as tee times can fill up quickly. Consider booking at least a month in advance to secure your preferred time. For the best deals, explore options that bundle green fees with accommodation. Also, keep an eye out for seasonal promotions and discounts offered on online booking platforms.

**Course Accessibility:**

Kagoshima Airport 36 CC operates as a semi-private club. While membership exists, the courses are generally open to the public, allowing non-members to book tee times. This accessibility makes it a popular choice for visiting golfers. However, certain tournaments and events may restrict public access on specific dates, so it's always best to check the club's calendar or inquire during booking.

**Course Highlights:**

The club features two distinct 18-hole courses: the "Airport Course" and the "Kirishima Course."

*   **Airport Course:** As the name suggests, this course offers stunning views of the nearby Kagoshima Airport. The layout is relatively flat, making it walkable, but strategic bunkering and water hazards demand precision. The fairways are generously wide, offering a forgiving experience for less experienced players. However, the undulating greens present a challenge for putting. The signature hole is often considered to be the par-4 18th, a dogleg right with a strategically placed water hazard guarding the green. A well-placed tee shot is crucial for a chance at par.

*   **Kirishima Course:** This course is significantly more challenging than the Airport Course, with undulating terrain, tighter fairways, and more strategically placed hazards. The Kirishima Course demands accuracy and strategic thinking. The elevation changes provide spectacular views of the surrounding Kirishima mountain range. This course is known for its challenging par-3s, requiring precise club selection. Many golfers consider the par-5 15th to be the signature hole. A long, uphill par 5, it requires three well-struck shots to reach the green in regulation.

Both courses are meticulously maintained, with lush fairways and impeccably manicured greens. The overall design philosophy emphasizes playability for a wide range of skill levels while still providing a challenging and rewarding experience for seasoned golfers.

**Facilities:**

The clubhouse at Kagoshima Airport 36 CC is well-appointed, offering a comfortable and welcoming atmosphere. The restaurant serves a variety of Japanese and Western dishes, with a focus on fresh, local ingredients. The pro shop is well-stocked with golf equipment, apparel, and souvenirs.

However, the true highlight of the facilities is the onsen (hot spring bath). After a round of golf, golfers can relax and rejuvenate in the traditional Japanese bath, known for its therapeutic properties. The onsen features both indoor and outdoor baths, offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape. This is a quintessential Japanese experience and a perfect way to unwind after a day on the course. The onsen facilities are generally very clean and well-maintained.

**Scenic Beauty:**

Kagoshima Airport 36 CC is situated in a region renowned for its natural beauty. The Kirishima mountain range provides a stunning backdrop for the courses. The best time to visit is during the spring (March-May) when the cherry blossoms are in bloom or during the autumn (September-November) when the foliage turns vibrant shades of red and gold. These seasons offer pleasant temperatures and breathtaking scenery. Even during the summer months, the elevation provides a welcome respite from the heat and humidity of lower altitudes.

The courses are surrounded by lush greenery and meticulously maintained gardens, creating a tranquil and serene atmosphere. Keep an eye out for local wildlife, including various bird species and perhaps even a glimpse of the elusive Japanese serow (a type of goat-antelope).

**Access:**

The most convenient way to reach Kagoshima Airport 36 CC is by flying into Kagoshima Airport (KOJ). From the airport, the club is approximately a 15-minute drive. Taxis are readily available at the airport, or you can rent a car.

If traveling from Kagoshima City, the club is approximately a 45-minute drive via the Kyushu Expressway. Take the Mizobe-Kagoshima Airport Interchange (溝辺鹿児島空港IC) and follow the signs to the club.

Public transportation options are limited. While there are buses that run from Kagoshima Airport to nearby towns, a taxi or rental car is necessary to reach the club itself.

**Final Verdict:**

Kagoshima Airport 36 CC offers an exceptional value proposition for golfers seeking a challenging yet accessible experience in a beautiful setting. Its proximity to Kagoshima Airport makes it an incredibly convenient destination, particularly for those with limited time. While it may not boast the prestige of some of Japan's more exclusive clubs, it provides a memorable golfing experience at a fraction of the cost. The combination of two distinct courses, excellent facilities (including the onsen), and stunning scenery makes Kagoshima Airport 36 CC a must-visit for any golf enthusiast traveling to Kyushu. I highly recommend it, especially for golfers looking for a convenient, affordable, and authentic Japanese golfing experience. It’s a hidden gem that deserves to be discovered.
