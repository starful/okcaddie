The Masters Amakusa Course is an **ocean-view Amakusa** destination in Kumamoto Prefecture—worth the trip if you want coastal scenery and a full resort-style day, not a quick add-on after a Kumamoto Castle morning.

## Quick facts

| **Style** | Coastal / championship resort flavor |
| **Extras** | Spa / dining often part of the trip story |
| **Visitor access** | Portal and resort booking channels |
| **Best months** | March–June, Oct–Nov |

## Who it's for

- Travelers already committing to **Amakusa** (not just downtown Kumamoto)
- Golfers who want **views + conditioned greens** and will pay resort-side pricing
- Groups that can spare transfer time across bridges / coastal roads

Skip if your only free morning is in central Kumamoto—the drive eats the day. Consider Aso or nearer city options instead.

## Course overview

Coastal wind is the main variable. Play conservative off the tee when fairways narrow toward the sea breeze; center-green on exposed approaches. Bunkers and slopes matter more than chasing every pin. Expect Japanese cart-day rhythm with clubhouse dining afterward.

If spa or lodging is part of your plan, book those in the same conversation as the tee time so schedules align.

## Green fees & booking

Ocean resort-adjacent pricing is not bargain Hokkaido weekday golf. **Planning ranges**:

- **Weekday packages**: often high-¥10,000s to mid-¥20,000s
- **Weekend / holiday**: ¥20,000s+ when meals and extras stack

Use [The Masters Amakusa booking](/booking/the_masters_amakusa_course_en). Confirm rental clubs and left-hand availability before you travel to the islands.

## Access

- **Air**: Amakusa (AXJ) for closest access when flights fit; otherwise Kumamoto (KMJ) + long car transfer via Amakusa highways/bridges
- From KMJ, treat it as a **substantial drive**—often around two hours class depending on route and traffic; do not cut it fine
- Rental car is the default for bags

Build the round as the day’s main event, not a squeezed side quest.

## Dress code & tips

- Collared shirt, tailored bottoms, soft spikes
- Wind layer and sunscreen on exposed holes
- Related: [Aso Resort Grandvrio](/course/aso_resort_grandvrio_en) · [Booking tips](/guide/booking-tips-japan)

## Bottom line

Amakusa Masters is for golfers searching **ocean-view Kyushu tee times** who accept the transfer. Book the live package, rent a car, and let wind—not pin flags—set strategy.
