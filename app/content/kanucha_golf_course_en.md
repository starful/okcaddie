**Access:**

Kanucha Golf Course is located in Nago, Okinawa.

*   **From Naha Airport (OKA):** The most convenient way to reach the course is by rental car. The drive from Naha Airport takes approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes to 2 hours, depending on traffic. Follow the Okinawa Expressway (toll road) north towards Nago. Exit at the Kyoda Interchange (許田IC) and follow the signs to Kanucha Bay Hotel & Villas, which houses the golf course.
*   **By Limousine Bus:** Alternatively, you can take a Limousine Bus from Naha Airport to Kanucha Bay Hotel & Villas. The journey takes approximately 2 hours and 30 minutes. Check the Limousine Bus schedules in advance, as services may be limited.
*   **By Taxi:** A taxi from Naha Airport is the least convenient and most expensive option. The fare is likely to be substantial, and the journey will take a similar amount of time as driving.

Once you arrive at Kanucha Bay Hotel & Villas, the golf course is easily accessible within the resort grounds.
